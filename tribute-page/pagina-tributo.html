Elon Musk in a interview

Elon Reeve Musk, FRS (Pretoria, June 28, 1971) is a South African-Canadian-American entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is the founder, CEO and CTO of SpaceX; CEO of Tesla Motors; vice president of OpenAI, founder and CEO of Neuralink, and co-founder and president of SolarCity. On January 7, 2021, with an estimated personal wealth of around 188.5 billion dollars, he became the richest person in the world.

His companies are

  • Tesla
  • Spacex
  • Solarcity
  • Neuralink
